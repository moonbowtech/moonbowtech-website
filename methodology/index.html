The Moonbow Opportunity Acceleration Framework

A systems-based approach for strengthening institutional research capability before proposal development begins.

From vision to institutional capability

Moonbow Tech applies a roadmap-centered methodology that links strategic research vision to measurable technical progress, dependency management, institutional coordination, and proposal maturity.

1

Vision

Clarify the research opportunity, institutional ambition, and intended research enterprise impact.

2

Figures of Merit

Define objective measures of technical progress and capability headroom.

3

Dependencies

Map cross-college, center, partner, data, staffing, and infrastructure dependencies before they become late-cycle risks.

4

Roadmaps

Link milestones, gates, maturation pathways, audit points, and pivot logic into a coherent institutional architecture.

5

Opportunity Acceleration

Advance strategic research opportunities toward coordinated, institution-ready initiatives.

Not proposal review. Proposal maturity architecture.

Proposal reviewers evaluate proposals. Moonbow Tech evaluates whether the research enterprise is producing proposals with sufficient roadmap maturity before those reviewers ever see them.
