Methodology

The Moonbow Opportunity Acceleration Framework

Moonbow Tech applies a systems-based approach to strengthening institutional research capability. Rather than focusing exclusively on proposal development, the framework emphasizes the institutional processes, technical planning, and organizational coordination that consistently produce competitive research opportunities.

Core Elements

Opportunity Acceleration

Research opportunities mature over time. Effective institutions identify, coordinate, and develop promising opportunities well before proposal preparation begins.

Technology Roadmapping

Technology roadmaps provide a common planning architecture linking long-term vision, measurable objectives, technical milestones, dependencies, and decision points.

Figures of Merit

Figures of Merit establish objective measures that guide technology development, evaluate alternatives, and support strategic decision-making throughout the research lifecycle.

Institutional Coordination

Complex research initiatives require coordination across multiple organizational boundaries, including faculty, departments, colleges, centers, and institutional leadership.

AI-Enabled Decision Support

Artificial intelligence should strengthen institutional decision-making rather than replace expert judgment. AI can assist with opportunity discovery, knowledge synthesis, technology assessment, roadmap development, dependency analysis, and institutional coordination.

Guiding Principles

  • Institutional capability is a strategic asset.
  • Technology roadmaps enable coordinated decision-making.
  • Measurable Figures of Merit improve technical planning.
  • Opportunity acceleration begins long before proposal writing.
  • Sustainable research competitiveness depends on coordinated institutional systems.
