About

Why Moonbow

Moonbow Tech LLC helps academic institutions strengthen enterprise-wide research capability through technology roadmapping, opportunity acceleration, and AI-enabled research strategy.

We believe sustained research competitiveness depends on institutional capability rather than isolated proposal efforts. Successful research enterprises require coordinated planning, measurable technical objectives, effective collaboration across departments, colleges, and research centers, and disciplined technology roadmapping.

Our Perspective

Moonbow Tech brings together perspectives shaped by academic research, advanced engineering, technology development, research strategy, business development, and collaboration with federal research organizations. This multidisciplinary perspective informs a systems-based approach to institutional capability, technology roadmapping, opportunity acceleration, and AI-enabled decision support.

Rather than viewing proposals as isolated deliverables, we view them as the outcome of coordinated institutional strategy, technical planning, measurable progress, and organizational capability.

Leadership

Sid Suryanarayanan, Ph.D.
Founder & Principal Consultant

Sid founded Moonbow Tech to help universities strengthen the institutional systems that consistently develop and advance competitive research opportunities. His work integrates systems engineering, technology roadmapping, artificial intelligence, and strategic planning into practical frameworks for research enterprise development.

Our Philosophy

We believe institutional capability is a strategic asset.

Universities that consistently develop competitive research opportunities do so through disciplined coordination, measurable technical progress, effective collaboration, and long-term planning.

Our objective is to help institutions build those capabilities in practical, measurable, and sustainable ways.

Moonbow Tech helps universities strengthen the systems that create competitive research opportunities—not simply the proposals that result from them.
